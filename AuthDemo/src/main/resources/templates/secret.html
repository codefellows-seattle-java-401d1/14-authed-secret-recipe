Secret Recipe: Honey Mustard Chicken

Serves 2

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs of boneless, skinless chicken breast
  • 1/4 cup mustard (ideally dijon, but any kind is fine)
  • 1 tablespoon of oil
  • 2 tablespoons of honey
  • 1 tsp thyme (chopped if fresh -best), dry is okay
  • salt and peper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees
  2. Mix all wet ingredients
  3. Lightly rinse and pat dry chicken breasts
  4. Cover chicken with wet ingredients
  5. Sprinkle thyme on top, just before putting in the oven
  6. Bake for approximately 20 minutes - check temperature of chicken starting at 15 minutes - don't take out until internal temperature is at least 140 degrees
  7. Let cool for approx 5 minutes
  8. Add salt and pepper to taste
  9. Serve sliced over salad, pasta, rice, or other favorite sides
